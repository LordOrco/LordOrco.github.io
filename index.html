About me

Exploring worlds through code has always been a passion of mine. Driven by a commitment to build meaningful, immersive experiences, I pursued my degree in Design and Videogame Development at Universidad Rey Juan Carlos (2021, Madrid, Spain).

During my studies, I collaborated with an incredible team, where I took on roles that allowed me to bridge communication across diverse fields such as programming, sound design, and 3D modeling. This collaboration honed my skills in coding while fostering an adaptable mindset essential for game development.

Along the way, I deepened my understanding of programming languages and tools, including Unity (C#), Python, C++, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, pushing myself to design more complex mechanics and systems. My projects have also led me to independently create games, where I developed core programming while my sister contributed 2D art.

Through these experiences, I have gained solid skills in code architecture, multiplayer networking, and interactive mechanics. My goal is to continue enhancing my expertise and build larger-scale, impactful projects.

Current project Deep Sea Dragon Dash

2 year individual project, 2023-2025

PROJECT OVERVIEW

In a post-apocalyptic world, following a mutagenic virus that affected all living beings, species had to adapt to the virus and evolve or die. In the case of the human race, one of the greatest losses occurred; with the disappearance of up to 80% of its population.

The story unfolds through Mia and her faithful pet Ruffus; who will embark on an adventure across Europe (from southern Spain, where their hometown is, to the north of EU) to save her little brother Luca. As he was kidnapped by a more advanced technological organization for a scientific purpose without anyone's permission.

However, we will not know much of what they are going to do to him at the beginning. But, during the development of the story, we will be more and more eager to reach him.

In this adventure, Ruffus will be the protector and MVP of our protagonist Mia (and the one we're going to develop to the end in this project). He will be our guardian, friend, mount, key to puzzles and much more. Combining the mutations of both of them, they will manage to uncover the secret of the collapse of the previous world while searching for Luca.

FEATURE ROADMAP

We're facing a post-apocalyptic environment in southern Spain. There is a decline in human population and the environment changes as irrigation systems stop working; going back to its natural steppe form. Maybe even more desert-like due to the rise in temperature in the world left by the society of yesteryear.

However, in more northern areas of Europe, the vegetation gains strength and invades the buildings of the past civilization.

As so, we create different thumbnails representing Spain in this case; as it is Mia's birthplace. We generate 4 different environments: the outskirts of a city, a Spanish forest of melojos and cork oaks (typical trees that give that dry feeling), some wheat fields and the interior of a city.

And so, we end up choosing the most descriptive Spanish environment: the wheat fields; specially as the abandoned tank hints to what could've happened in the past.
